26.06.2013. Novi Sad Check out the location on the map

You'll meet people with successful companies and web projects. Whether it's about designing user interfaces, scaling Software as a Service products or selling to tens of thousands of customers, what sets them apart is not just that they have good ideas, but know how to execute them. You won't just be inspired — you'll hear how things are actually done.

Focus

How successful engineers and designers work, execute their ideas and run their businesses. And of course — getting to know each other and having a good time.

Speakers

Darko Fabijan

Darko is a partner at Rendered Text, a software company specialized for web application development. He's dedicated to building an exceptional team and is totally in the "ship it" mindset.

@darkofabijan

Goran Bajazetov

Goran is an UI and UX designer and creative director at Saturized, an agency making digital products and services. He's committed to crafting great interfaces, connecting creative people and a quality life.

@bajazetov

Janko Jovanović

Janko is an interaction designer with 20 years of experience in designing and developing digital products and services. He is 1/2 of Studio At Warp Speed, a tiny design agency that helps businesses shape their products and services. In his free time, he runs a blog about design jankoatwarpspeed.com and speaks regularly at major local events.

@jankowarpspeed

Marko Anastasov

Marko is a partner at Rendered Text and currently dedicated to their service Semaphore, where he balances product development, tech support and writing code.

@markoa

Milica Jovanović

Milica is an interaction designer with more than 10 years of experience in designing, developing and managing large-scale software projects. She is the prettiest part of Studio At Warp Speed, a small design agency that she runs with her husband. As a former developer, manager, Scrum master and lecturer, she has broad interest in all aspects of product design and effectively uses her previous knowledge today.

@leonissima

Petar Perović

Petar Perović works as a freelance designer. In the past he was part of a startup Recordr, cofounder of Superawesome agency and Plakatt web site. He has a special interest in letters and typography.

@ropsii

Siniša Komlenić

Siniša is a serial entrepreneur: he launched Themes Kingdom, a premium WordPress theme development and shopping company; Tickera, a ticket sales and distribution system, and Train'rs, a web tool for professional trainers through a project "10 days for a startup". He's active in the local startup community as a mentor and speaker.

@sinisakomlenic
